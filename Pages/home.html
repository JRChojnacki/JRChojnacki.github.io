11.03.2021
Finite Action Prinicple in Horava-Lifshitz Gravity
The manuscript
Finite Action Principle and Horava-Lifshitz Gravity: early universe, black holes and wormholes
has appeard on ArXiv. We find that the Finite Action Principle resolves singularity problem in Horava-Lifshitz gravity. Singular black hole solutions do not contribute path integral, however, the worm holes seem to be allowed in the finite action context.

PLANCKS 2021 competition
My teammates and I won the Polish preliminary of
PLANCKS 2021
competition. The four of us are going to represent Poland during the remote 36-hour-long international tournament. This edition of PLANCKS is going to be unlike any other- any online resource may be used during solving 12 extremely difficult problems. The problems cover a broad spectrum of theoretical physics. Asymptotically safe eternal inflation
Today I have published my first preprint
"Is asymptotically safe inflation eternal?"
. Together with my collegues we have worked on this project for the past 8 months. Our results shed light on the creation of the multiverse in inflationary theories with metastable vacua.
